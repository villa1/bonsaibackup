ABOUT US

Indonesia's Premier Bonsai & Tropical Plant Supplier

Since 2005, Indo Cipanas Bonsai has been cultivating exceptional bonsai and tropical plants in the highlands of West Java, Indonesia. Our 5-hectare nursery complex in Cipanas combines traditional Indonesian techniques with modern horticultural practices to produce specimens of outstanding quality and beauty.

What began as a small family business has grown into a respected international supplier, serving clients in over 30 countries. Our team includes master bonsai artists with decades of experience, horticulturists specializing in rare tropical species, and logistics experts who ensure seamless international delivery.

We're proud to maintain strong relationships with local farming communities, supporting sustainable cultivation practices that benefit both people and the environment.
